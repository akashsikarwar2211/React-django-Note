Uske Paas Jaao

Jo tumhe samajhne ki koshish kare,
Chahe tum khud ko samjha na pao…
Uske paas jaao.

Jo tumhari khamoshi ko bhi padh le,
Bina ek shabd ke samajh le…
Uske paas jaao.

Jo tumhara bojh baant le,
Chahe tumne kabhi maanga na ho…
Uske paas jaao.

Jab bheed mein khud ko akela pao,
Aur saans bhi bojh lage…
Uske paas jaao.

Pata nahi kyun, yeh dil hamesha bhaari rehta hai.
Kuch chahiye nahi, phir bhi ek intezaar sa laga rehta hai…
Sapne kuch aur the, socha kuch aur tha…
Aur ho kuch aur raha hai.

Na pichhe jaa sakta hoon,
Na aage badh pa raha hoon.
Bas ek kone mein baitha rehta hoon,
Jaise khud se bhi nazar milane ki himmat kho chuka hoon.

Parivaar ka sahara banna tha,
Par khud ek sahara banne ki talash mein hoon.
Shayad zindagi aisi hi hai…
Jo hum chahte hain, wo aksar nahi hota.

"Chhodo… zyada sochne ka kya faida.
Phone rakhte hain…
Aur chup-chaap so jaate hain."
